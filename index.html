Ham Sandwich

  • Time: 4 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Deli Ham 3 slices
Bread 2 slices
Mustard Too Taste
Mayo Too Taste

Step 1: Chose good bread.

Step 2: Spread Mayo on one slice and Mustard on the other slice.

Step 3: Stack Ham and Cheese.

Step 4: Sandwich them together and Enjoy.

Source: Taste of Home

Chicken Cup Noodles

  • Time: 3 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Cup Noodle 1
Water 1 cup

Step 1: Remove film and cardboard.

Step 2: Peel top halfway.

Step 3: Fill water to the line.

Step 4: Microwave 3 minutes and Enjoy.

Source: Cup Noodle

Salmon Nigiri

  • Time: 4 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi grade Salmon 1 fillet
Sushi rice 1 cup

Step 1:Prepare rice.

Step 2: Slice salmon fillet into thin slices.

Step 3: Make small balls of rice.

Step 4: Place cuts of salmon onto rice balls and Enjoy.

Source: Izzy Cooking
